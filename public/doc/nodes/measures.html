Measures

Measure distance and angles

This module provides three types of measurement:

Atoms
Clicking (Ctrl-Click) on one to three atoms gives their coordinates, distances and angle.
Polyhedra
Clicking (Ctrl-Click) on a polyhedra gives its volume.
Bonds
Clicking (Ctrl-Click) on an atom gives the lengths of its bonds.

The results table shows, when the measurement type Atom is selected:

  • Atoms type and their coordinates. These lines are colored like the atoms selection marker.
  • Distances between pair or atoms.
  • Angle (in degrees) created by the three atoms.
  • The information provided depends on the number of atoms selected. If more than three atoms are selected the first entered will be deselected. Selecting again a selected atom deselects it.

The results table shows, when the measurement type Polyhedra is selected:

  • When a polyhedra is selected by Ctrl-Click, its color changes and its volume is shown.

The results table shows, when the measurement type Bonds is selected:

  • The bonded atom index and its bond length. The bonds length are also put near the corresponding bond in the viewer3D.

Controls

Chart type radio button Selects the type of measurement to make: Atoms, Polyhedra, Bonds
Show fractional coordinates switch This control appears only if Atoms measurement is selected. If set, the atoms coordinates shown change from cartesian to fractional.
Results table Depending on the type of measurement, here the measurement results appear.
Deselect button Deselect selected atoms and polyhedra.
