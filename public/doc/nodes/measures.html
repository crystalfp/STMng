Measures

Measure distance and angles

This module provides three types of measurements and a summary of the current structure.

The structure summary shows:

  • Step: The step of the current visualized structure.
  • Atom count: The total number of atoms subdivided by atom type in the following lines.
  • Bonds count: The number of bonds present and, if present, how may of them are H bonds.

The unit cell summary shows:

  • Sides: The sides of the unit cell (a, b, c).
  • Angles: The angles (in degrees) of the unit cell (α, β, γ).
  • Origin: The origin of the unit cell (x, y, z).

The module provides three types of measurements:

Atoms
Clicking (Ctrl-Click) on one to three atoms gives their coordinates, distances and angle.
Polyhedra
Clicking (Ctrl-Click) on a polyhedra gives its volume.
Bonds
Clicking (Ctrl-Click) on an atom gives the lengths of its bonds.

The results table shows, when the measurement type Atom is selected:

  • Atoms type and their coordinates. These lines are colored like the atoms selection marker.
  • Distances between pair or atoms.
  • Angle (in degrees) created by the three atoms.
  • The information provided depends on the number of atoms selected. If more than three atoms are selected the first one will be deselected. Selecting again a selected atom deselects it.

The results table shows, when the measurement type Polyhedra is selected:

  • When a polyhedra is selected by Ctrl-Click, its color changes and its volume is shown.

The results table shows, when the measurement type Bonds is selected:

  • The bonded atom index and its bond length. The bonds length are also put near the corresponding bond in the viewer3D.

Controls

Structure summary table Here the structure summary appears.
Unit cell table If the structure has an unit cell, here its summary appears.
Measurement type radio button Selects the measurement to make: Atoms, Polyhedra or Bonds
Show fractional coordinates toggle This control appears only if Atoms measurement is selected and the structure has unit cell. If set, the atoms coordinates shown change from cartesian to fractional.
Results table Depending on the type of measurement, here the measurement results appear.
Deselect button Deselect selected atoms and polyhedra.
