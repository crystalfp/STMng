Slice structure

Remove part of the structure

Remove part of a structure using a sphere, a plane or a pair of planes. Also specific atoms or specific atoms plus directly bonded atoms could be directly selected for removal by symbol, label or index. The removed part could be inside or outside the selected geometry or list of atoms.

No geometry appears to show the slice when directly select atoms for removal.

The spheres that delimit the cut are centered on the selected atoms.

The plane that delimits the cut is selected by giving its intersect with the basis vectors and/or the basis vector(s) that should be parallel to the plane. The intersections could also be given with Miller indices (hkl).

A pair of planes with the same distance from the selected plane delimits a slab that cuts the structure.

If the geometric parameters make no sense (e.g. plane parallel to three axis) an error is shown.

Controls

Enable slicer toggle Enable the structure slicing.
Show slicer geometry toggle Show the slicer geometry independently from the effective slicing of the structure. This is disabled if the selection is direct, without showing the geometry.
Slice inside toggle Reverse the side of the removed part of the structure.
Mode list Select the geometry that cuts the structure or by direct selection of the atoms.
Plane
The cutting geometry is a plane.
Miller
The cutting geometry is a plane determined by its Miller indices.
Sphere
The cutting geometries are spheres centered on selected atoms.
Slab
The slab is determined by two parallel planes at a given distance.
Direct
The atoms to cut are selected directly by symbol, label or index.
Bonded
Atoms are selected directly by symbol, label or index and them plus their direct bonded atoms are removed.
Various controls various controls Position of the cutting geometry.
Plane
For each base vector there is a toggle that determine if the plane is parallel to the vector and a slider that fix the intersection with the vector as a percentage of the vector length.
Miller
Three sliders set the Miller (hkl) indices. Another slider sets the offset of the plane from the computed one.
Sphere
An atom selector selects the center atom of the spheres. A slider sets the common radius.
Slab
The two planes delimiting the slab are equidistant from a plane defined as in the “Plane” mode. The slab planes are distant half the set thickness from the defined plane.
Direct and Bonded
An atom selector to select the atoms.
Reset parameters button Reset the geometry parameters to a sane default.
