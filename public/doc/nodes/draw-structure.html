Structure

Draw structure

Render a structure to the screen.

Controls

Structure rendering mode radio buttons How to render the structure.
CPK
Atoms are represented with a radius proportional to their covalent radii.
VdW
Atoms are represented with a radius proportional to their Van der Waals radii. No bonds are rendered.
Licorice
Atoms are represented with a radius equal to the bond radius.
Lines
Bonds are rendered by lines. Atoms are not rendered.
Smooth color bonds toggle Bonds are colored by a smooth transition between the bonded atoms colors. Otherwise there are only two color bands. This not applies to line rendering.
Label is radio buttons What represents the label on each atom.
Symbol
The atom type symbol.
Label
The atom label eventually present in the input structure.
Index
Index of the atom inside the structure. The index starts from zero.
Visibility toggle buttons Selects what is rendered.
  • Structure
  • Bonds
  • Labels
Quality radio buttons Select the quality of the rendering by varying the subdivisions of spheres and cylinders.
  • Low
  • Medium
  • Good
  • Best
Roughness slider Roughness of the surfaces.
Metalness slider Metal-like shining of the surfaces.
