Viewer

Viewer 3D controls

Interface to the 3D viewer. Its parameters are grouped under 5 categories:

  • Camera positioning
  • Scene
  • Ambient light
  • Directional lights
  • Helper objects

Controls

Camera type radio button Selects the type of camera: Perspective or Orthographic.
Camera positioning group Enable saving and setting the camera position to have reproducible view of the structure.
Scene group Select the viewer background color.
Ambient light group Select the ambient light color and intensity.
Directional lights group Select color, intensity and direction for three directional lights. The direction is specified by rotation around X, Y, Z axis and around a direction perpendicular to the given axis.
Helper objects group Enable five helper objects:
  • Axis: the cartesian axis from the space origin with setting of the axis length.
  • Grids: along XZ, XY and YZ planes with setting of their side.
  • Orientation axis: Widget that show the camera orientation.
