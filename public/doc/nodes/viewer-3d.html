Viewer

Viewer 3D controls

Controls the 3D viewer parameters. These are grouped under eight collapsable categories as detailed below.

Controls

Camera type group A radio button that selects the type of camera: Perspective or Orthographic. The selected type is displayed in the group label.
Camera positioning group Enable saving, restoring and setting the camera position to make possible reproducible visualizations.
Camera position auto reset group Enable the camera position reset on every new structure visualized.
Scene group Select the viewer background color.
Depth cueing group Enables the viewer depth cueing (also called fog) and selects the near and far distances for the effect.
Ambient light group Select the ambient light color and intensity.
Directional lights group Select color, intensity and direction for three directional lights. The direction is specified by rotation around X, Y, Z axis and around a direction perpendicular to the given axis. Setting intensity to zero deactivates the corresponding light.
Helper objects group Enable five helper objects:
  • Axis: the cartesian axis centered on the space origin with a setting of the axis length.
  • Grids: planes through the origin along XZ, XY and YZ axis with a setting of their side length.
  • Orientation axis: widget that show the camera orientation and enables its manipulation.
Restore viewer settings button Restore the viewer settings to the factory defaults.
