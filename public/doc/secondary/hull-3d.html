STMng — Variable composition 3D convex hull window

For a variable composition of 3 components this window shows the convex hull in 3D. X and Y coordinates are the same found in the 2D convex hull chart and Z is the energy of formation.

The window provide two sliders to change the Z scale of the convex hull and the dimension of the points.
