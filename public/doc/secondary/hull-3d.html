STMng — Variable composition 3D convex hull window

Controls

Scale slider Change the Z scale of the convex hull. This slider is disabled for 4 components convex hull.
Point size slider Change the dimension of the points.
Center button Recenter the view to be along the Z axis.
Show legend toggle Show the legend for the points color.
Colormap selector Name of the colormap to convert values into colors.
Color by radio buttons Selects value to be represented as color of the points.
Formation
The points are colored by their enthalpy of formation.
Distance
The points are colored by their distance from the convex hull.
Show lines toggle Show the edges of the convex hull.
Show labels toggle Show composition of the points on the convex hull.
Close button Close the window.

Clicking Ctrl-Left button on the points reveals a panel with the point data. Clicking on the background hides the panel.

Variable composition with 3 components

For a variable composition of 3 components this window shows the convex hull in 3D. X and Y coordinates are the same found in the 2D convex hull chart and Z is the enthalpy of formation.

Variable composition with 4 components

For 4 components only the points are shown, because the convex hull is composed by 4D tetrahedra.

The only difference with the 3 components visualization is the scale slider disabled because the energy axis is not visible in 3D. Remember the points live in 4 dimensions: three for the composition (the four percentages should sum to 1 hence the 3) and one for the energy.
