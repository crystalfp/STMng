STMng — Energy-volume convex hull window

The window show the structure energy vs. cell volume and the lower part of the convex hull. Hovering on the points show a popup with information on the corresponding structure.

Controls

Color by radio buttons Selects value to be represented as color of the points.
None
All points are colored burnt orange.
Distance
The points are colored by their energy distance from the convex hull.
Show legend toggle Show the legend for the points color. Disabled if the points have color “None”.
Colormap selector Name of the colormap to convert values into colors.
Close button Close the window.
