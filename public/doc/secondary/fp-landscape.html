STMng — Fingerprints energy landscape window

Show the per atom energy surface interpolating the computed fingerprints projected to 2D and the corresponding energies.

Scale slider Vertical scale of the surface.
Grid side slider Side subdivisions of the resulting square surface. It is a power of two.
Power slider Parameter for the interpolator. Values bigger than two (the default) make the points influence be more local to a given point. Less than two and the points and melted together.
Colormap selector Name of the colormap to convert values into colors.
Show points toggle If set, the true position of the fingerprints is show with a point. Note that these points do not rest exactly on the surface because this last is an interpolation of the points on a regular grid. The difference could be reduced by increasing the grid side.
Close button Close the window.
