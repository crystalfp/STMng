STMng — atom data provenance

The atom data used to render the atoms is from OpenBabel element.txt file. STMng does not uses all the fields provided.

The bonds strength values are from the goodBonds table provided by USPEX computational material discovery system. Their initial proposal is from: Andriy O. Lyakhov and Artem R. Oganov, “Evolutionary search for superhard materials: Methodology and applications to forms of carbon and TiO2”, Phys. Rev. B 84, 092103 – Published 12 September, 2011, DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevB.84.092103.
